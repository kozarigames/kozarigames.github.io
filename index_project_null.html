PROJECT NULL


You wake up in a dark forest and you don't know how you got here, and you don't remember anything. But somehow everything changed. You meet strange creatures, and no one is very friendly in this alien world. Soldiers, monsters and basically everything in this place is trying to end your journey quickly. Are you up to the task? Are you ready? Explore this new world full of HORROR and SCI-FI elements!

Use your brain! Or your gun!

- Sometimes it's better to just run away, but other times it's better to use your brain, solve logic puzzles, or just simply use your laser weapon to defeat your opponents. Of course, action isn't always the best thing to do at the moment.

- You find yourself in a completely new world, you must find out where you ended up and who you are.

- Everything is covered with the strangest creatures, horror, blood in all quantities, sci-fi... that's what awaits you.

- Pick-up items that you can use during the game. You can interact with game elements, and you can do climbing, crawling, swimming and whatever else you can imagine.
